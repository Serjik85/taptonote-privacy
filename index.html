Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 31, 2025

Tap To Note does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

The app works entirely on your devices (Apple Watch and iPhone). All notes are saved locally and are not sent to any servers or third-party services.

We do not:

  • Collect or access your name, email, or location
  • Track your activity
  • Use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs

Data synchronization happens only via Apple’s built-in technologies (e.g., iCloud or device sync), and is governed by Apple’s privacy policies.

If you have any questions, feel free to contact the developer at: serhii@tkachenko.guru

Thank you for using Tap To Note! We believe privacy is a basic right. ✌️
